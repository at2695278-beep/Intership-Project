Spider-Man: Homecoming is about Peter Parker balancing school life with being a young superhero. He tries to prove himself worthy beyond just a “friendly neighborhood” hero. Facing the Vulture, he learns responsibility and what it truly means to be Spider-Man.

Spider-Man: Far From Home takes Peter Parker on a school trip to Europe after Endgame. He wants a normal life, but gets pulled into fighting Mysterio’s illusions. The movie shows him stepping up as a true hero while dealing with Tony Stark’s legacy.
